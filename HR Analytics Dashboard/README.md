# Madhav E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

## Overview
This Power BI project presents an interactive **E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** designed to analyze sales performance, profit trends, customer behavior, and payment patterns across different states and categories.

The dashboard helps in understanding business growth and identifying key areas for improvement through clear visual insights.

---

## Key Features

- Total Sales Amount Analysis
- Total Quantity Sold Tracking
- Profit Monitoring
- Average Sales Performance
- State-wise Sales Distribution
- Category-wise Quantity Analysis
- Monthly Profit Trends
- Customer-wise Sales Contribution
- Payment Mode Analysis
- Sub-Category Profit Breakdown
- Quarter and State Filters for Dynamic Analysis

---

## Dashboard Insights

### KPIs
- **Total Amount:** 438K
- **Total Quantity:** 5615
- **Total Profit:** 37K
- **Average Sales:** 121K

### Sales by State
Top performing states include:
- Maharashtra
- Madhya Pradesh
- Uttar Pradesh
- Delhi

### Category Performance
- Clothing contributes the highest quantity sold
- Electronics and Furniture follow next

### Profit Trends
- Highest profit observed in **November**
- Negative profit seen in **May, July, August, October, and December**

### Payment Modes
Most preferred payment mode:
- COD (Cash on Delivery)

Other payment methods:
- UPI
- Debit Card
- Credit Card
